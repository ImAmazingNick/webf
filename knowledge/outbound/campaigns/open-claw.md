# Campaign: Open Claw for Business

> **Concept:** Developers got Claude Code — an AI that sees the full codebase and operates across it. Businesses got... dashboards. Open Claw is the campaign that says: the same leap that happened in dev tooling is happening in business operations. Improvado is that leap.
> **Campaign name:** Open Claw (a nod to the open, cross-functional nature — the AI "claw" that reaches across every data silo and grabs the full picture)
> **Tagline:** The full-context AI that business teams deserve.

---

## The Narrative

### The Setup (what everyone knows)

Developers went from autocomplete → Copilot → Claude Code.

The leap wasn't smarter autocomplete. It was **full codebase context**. Claude Code sees every file, every dependency, every pattern. That's what makes it a 10x engineer — not better text prediction.

### The Gap (what nobody's saying)

Business teams are still in the autocomplete era.

- Your ad platform sees ads. Not pipeline. Not revenue.
- Your CRM sees deals. Not product usage. Not support tickets.
- Your BI tool sees whatever you manually feed it. And it's already stale.

Every business tool is Copilot — smart in one file, blind to the codebase.

### The Thesis (what Improvado is)

Improvado is Open Claw — the full-context AI for business.

One knowledge graph that connects **all** business data: marketing, sales, support, product, finance, operations. One AI agent that reasons across all of it and takes action.

**Claude Code : codebase :: Improvado Agent : business**

The knowledge graph is the codebase. The AI agent is the 10x operator.

## Tone Rules for Open Claw

- **Never disparage Claude Code or any AI tool.** The analogy is aspirational, not competitive. "Developers got something amazing. Business teams deserve the same."
- **The hero is context, not AI.** The insight is that full context is the breakthrough — not smarter models.
- **Keep it concrete.** Every time you say "full context," follow with a specific example.
- **No banned words:** revolutionary, game-changing, AI-powered, best-in-class
