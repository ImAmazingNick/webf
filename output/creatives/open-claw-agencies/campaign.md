## Visual Philosophy: Command Luminance

A premium agency command center — the vantage point of one AI operator that sees
every client at once. Multiple client dashboards glow in the deep purple darkness,
each showing real marketing metrics, but the commanding view belongs to one agent
that monitors them all simultaneously. The environment is near-black with deep
purple undertones, violet UI element accents, and one mint-green highlight on the
key insight or metric.

Headlines and CTAs are rendered directly into the visual by Grok Imagine — bold,
clean white typography integrated into the dark premium scene. The text feels native
to the environment, as if projected onto glass surfaces or floating in the command
center space. The logo is composited separately at SVG quality by the agent HTML layer.
