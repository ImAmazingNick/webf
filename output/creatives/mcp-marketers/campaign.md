## Visual Philosophy: The Universal Connector

MCP is the bridge between AI and every marketing tool. Before: fragmented
APIs, custom integrations, siloed data. After: one protocol, every tool,
one AI agent that reasons across all of it. The visual language is
connection — streams converging, fragments unifying, scattered light
focusing into one beam. The Improvado knowledge graph as the foundation,
MCP as the universal key.
