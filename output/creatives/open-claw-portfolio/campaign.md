## Visual Philosophy: Holographic Command

A cinematic sci-fi command center where one AI agent has total visibility across
an entire agency portfolio. Floating holographic screens, translucent data panels,
luminous data streams — the visual language of a future where one operator sees
everything simultaneously. The environment is deep purple-black void with dramatic
volumetric lighting from holographic projections in violet and mint. Every frame
should feel like a still from a $200M sci-fi film, not a SaaS ad.

Headlines and CTAs are rendered directly by Grok Imagine as bold white typography
integrated into the scene. The logo is composited separately at SVG quality.
